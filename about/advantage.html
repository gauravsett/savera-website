Savera-Doctus Advantage

Savera ownership maintains a controlling interest in Doctus Data Services Private Limited in Hyderabad, India, a premier offshore fulfillment center:

ISO, SOC II, HITRUST Certification

  • Doctus Data Services Private Limited in Hyderabad, India is an ISO 27001:2013, SOC II Type II certified company.

Privacy & Security Peace of Mind

  • Our privacy and security measures and safeguards achieve compliance with HIPAA Legislation, PHI, PII and NIST guidelines, and protect sensitive information.
  • Digital, paperless production infrastructure. Work is performed in the client’s native systems. Patient data is neither download nor stored in our systems to perform the work. Our work quality and productivity are fully transparent to our clients.

Team Extension Efficacy and Process Scale

  • We enable sustainable change by delivering customized revenue cycle and clinical/ financial analytics services that support our client’s unique organizational needs.

Experienced, Dedicated, On-shore and Off-shore Workforce

  • We only deploy experienced on-shore and off-shore associates.

SLA Based Annuity Client Partnership.

  • We encourage quarterly contract performance review and SLA resets to share productivity and gains. Our attractive and competitive pricing structure improves client cost spend.

Partnership

  • We partner with hospitals and health systems, physician practices and related services organizations to provide a broad portfolio of customizable solutions, to deliver measurable, meaningful results, improve productivity and quality, and reduce cost of service.

Option to Monetize Investment

  • Our clients have option to acquire the Unit dedicated to their operations at pre-determined rate.
